SimCity

This is the homepage of the SimCity front-end project. This project is developed by the Distributed Computing students at the University of Antwerp.

The following functionality is available in the current version:

  • User management
  • Worker management (currently disabled due to the use of a single worker machine)
  • Bot management of the following simulated bot types:
    • Car (robot)
    • Drone
    • F1-car
  • Terminal service
